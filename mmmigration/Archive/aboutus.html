Mission Statement

At MMM we are passionate about helping our clients to achieve their dream of settling in a new country with the best possible opportunities, whilst providing the  highest level of professional service at the most competitive price. 

Our vision is to assist you in making your dream come true and to achieve your highest potential in Australia, land of opportunity, the perfect environment to create a wonderful life for yourself and your family. Likewise for our employers we seek to provide highly skilled applicants who will contribute to your company’s growth. 

We will help you every step of the way.  

Background

Melanie Macfarlane, Registered Migration Agent MARN 0319166
Melanie Macfarlane’s career spans more than 13 years, working in Australia, Indonesia and the UK, in both the education and migration industries.  Melanie spent 5 years working in England as Marketing Manager at a large English language school, travelling extensively throughout Asia.  Since achieving High Distinction in the MARA exam to become a Migration Agent in 2003 in Sydney, Australia, she has been working as a Migration Agent and Education Consultant, successfully obtaining visas for hundreds of applicants from countries all over the world.  Melanie is university educated with a BA (Hons) in Modern Languages in Russian and French and in addition speaks Spanish and Indonesian. 

Carlos Alberto Useche Cruz, Colombia Representative
Carlos has a background as a teacher in Colombia and since April 2008 has been working as an Education Consultant for MMM’s sister company, Viva Estudie y Trabaje en Australia (VETA) advising clients on which study path to follow and how this will assist them in their future career.
