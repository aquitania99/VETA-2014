Employment

MMM works with Nucleus Recruitment to place its clients in suitable positions in the construction, engineering, mining and hospitality industries.

Suitable applicants can travel and work in Australia, fully paid, for up to 4 years on a Temporary Work Visa. This is an employer sponsored visa for occupations and skills which are in demand in Australia.
Are you eligible to apply?


Engineers, construction and mining professionals:

  • Recognised relevant bachelors degree
  • Minimum of 2-3 years relevant work experience
  • Experience in the mining industry is highly regarded but not essential
  • Proficiency in English

Tradespeople:

  • Recognised trade certificate or equivalent
  • Minimum 3 years work experience
  • Proficiency in English

Culinary Arts:

  • Recognised trade certificate, diploma or degree
  • 3-4 years work experience
  • Proficiency in English
  • Not enough experience? Find out more about a fully paid internship program through our partner company Internship Company

Nurses

  • Recognised nursing qualification from a university as well as minimum of two years work experience and IELTS 6.5 in all bands
