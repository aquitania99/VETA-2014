Internships

MMM works with one of its partner companies Internship Company to provide internships to recent graduates of hospitality institutions.

If you a recent culinary arts or hospitality graduate with less than 2 years work experience who would like a fully paid internship in Australia then apply here.

Internship Company offers a paid 12 month internship program for candidates who would like to receive additional training, experience and exposure to the English language through working in a hotel, resort or restaurant in Australia.

To qualify candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • Recent graduate of a hospitality or gastronomy program (minimum 12 months in duration)
  • Less than 2 years work experience (including course attachments)
  • Proficiency in English with a minimum score of IELTS 5.5 or equivalent
