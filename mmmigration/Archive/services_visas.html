Visas

MMM provides a range of visa options, according to your own specific requirements and background. 

Australian immigration policy is highly complex and is subject to constant change.  At MMM we are constantly updating our knowledge in order to provide the most efficient service to you, our client.  Our level of experience enables us to prepare your application in the correct way so that it meets the requirements of the Department of Immigration and can be processed as quickly as possible. 

The following represent the visas that we will process on your behalf:

  1. General Skilled Migration
  2. Regional Skilled Migration
  3. Employer Sponsorship – for Australian companies employing overseas workers
  4. Temporary Work Visas – for overseas workers to work in Australia
  5. Occupational Trainee Visa - for overseas workers to undertake a period of training in Australia
  6. Partner Visas – spouse, fiancée, interdependent
  7. Offshore and Onshore Student visas
  8. E-visa services
  9. Business skills visas
